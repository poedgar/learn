Час (Tense) Тип речення 1. Звичайне дієслово (Regular Verb) 2. Дієслово "бути" (Verb "to be")
Present Simple Розповідне I / We / They live here.
He / She / It lives here. 		I am here.
He / She / It is here.
We / They are here.
Заперечувальне I / We / They do not (don't) live here.
He / She / It does not (doesn't) live here. 		I am not here.
He / She / It is not (isn't) here.
We / They are not (aren't) here.
Запитальне Do I / we / they live here?
Does he / she / it live here? 		Am I here?
Is he / she / it here?
Are we / they here?
Past Simple Розповідне I / He / She / It / We / They lived here. I / He / She / It was here.
We / They were here.
Заперечувальне I / He / She / It / We / They did not (didn't) live here. I / He / She / It was not (wasn't) here.
We / They were not (weren't) here.
Запитальне Did I / he / she / it / we / they live here? Was I / he / she / it here?
Were we / they here?
Future Simple Розповідне I / He / She / It / We / They will live here. I / He / She / It / We / They will be here.
Заперечувальне I / He / She / It / We / They will not (won't) live here. I / He / She / It / We / They will not (won't) be here.
Запитальне Will I / he / she / it / we / they live here? Will I / he / she / it / we / they be here?
